In Game Shop #1

Hello survivors! This tutorial is for those new to the server.

The first step is to find the store. Each city has one, marked in.

You need a wallet, which can be found in a zombie's pocket. Once you have it, place it in your main inventory (cardboard box), right-click and press "Link" to connect it to your bank account.

To access the in-game store, go to the merchant, right-click, and select "Shop".

Items that can be sold include jewelry, drugs, cigarettes, zombie ears, and watches. These items are from the 2024 season and may change over time.

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning! Selling more than 1500-2000 copper coins at once may cause game lag.

In Game Shop #2

Hello survivors! This store can be accessed anywhere from the game's pause menu (press "ESC").

You can buy event coins and cars with points earned by spending time on the server. The more active you are, the more points you get. Points appear in the upper-right corner.

After buying event coins, use a wallet, right-click the coins, and select "Move Coins to Account" to add them to your bank account.

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning! Buying a car will spawn it where you are. Do not buy in a building‚Äîuse a free area such as a street or parking lot.

How to Claim a Car

To own a car only you (or your faction members, if applicable) can access, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the store and obtain a "Vehicle Claim Orb".
  2. Buy it.
  3. Equip the orb in your hand, right-click the car, and select "Claim Vehicle".
  4. Claimed vehicles appear in the "Claimed Vehicles Manager", accessible anywhere by right-clicking.

‚ö†Ô∏è If the orb gets stuck, re-enter the game.

How to Claim a House

To own a house:

  1. Survive 5 days with your character.
  2. Find a house. Owned houses are marked in light brown on the map.
  3. Ensure the house is empty, enter it, right-click inside, and press "Claim Safehouse".

‚ö†Ô∏è Choose a square-shaped house to fully cover the protected area.

New Roles and How to Obtain Them

Rank up by being active and engaging with the community. Each role requires time spent on the server and messages sent.

  • Crusader: 1 month, 100 messages
  • Scavenger: 2 months, 400 messages
  • Tracker: 5 months, 1000 messages
  • Sentinel: 7 months, 1950 messages
  • Overseer: 10 months, 3350 messages
  • Veterans: 12 months, 4950 messages

General Tips: Stay engaged, participate in events, and use !show_info to track stats.

How to Move/Deactivate Coordinates Info

To move coordinates, press the "Right Alt" key and drag with your mouse. Release when positioned.

To remove coordinates, double-click the three orange lines; the overlay will disappear.

Skills Journal

Create a skill recovery journal to save your progress. After leveling up a skill, write it in the journal. If you die, the journal restores 70% of the accumulated experience.
